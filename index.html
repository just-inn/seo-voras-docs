Article Submission Automation System

About

Automated system for sending article placement requests to publishers and tracking invoices.

Architecture

Each employee has their own Google Sheet file (e.g., Jonas, Ona). They fill in article info and upload Word + JPG files to their own Drive folder.

Employee Sheet

Fill in info, mark status as "Complete" when ready

Master Sheet

Auto-pulls "Complete" rows. Monthly tabs (Dec, Jan...)

No copy-pasting between tabs - just change the status. Laura reviews in Master Sheet before sending.

2. Spam Protection

Gmail can block if too many emails sent at once. Script sends gradually:

  • 20-30 emails per hour
  • Random pause between each (30-90 sec)
  • Progress visible in Sheet
Queued: 45 Sent: 12 Remaining: 33

First month recommended to start slower (10-15/hour), gradually increase.

3. Tracking

After sending, each employee's Sheet automatically updates:

  • Status = "Sent"
  • Date and time
  • Direct link to sent email in Gmail
